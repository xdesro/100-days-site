100 Days of Making

Ninety-nine plus one days of making something every day.

The rules are relatively simple —

every day has to be creation. Whether it's design or code,

I will have created something new by the end of the day. That does not include refactoring code, renaming layers, etc.

rule two

is that everything must serve a purpose. I won't allow myself to design or build something just for the sake of designing.

(Full disclosure, and you’ll see this, but I added this rule after day 1.)
